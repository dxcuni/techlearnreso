Introduction

Python CodeX Practice Lab program is created to provide developers with some programming experience coming from another language to Python, or existing Python developers looking to up-skill, courses other supplemental learning

 

The full program consists of four Python Fundamentals courses followed by Python CodeX Practice Lab

We have also recommended a host of optional resources that goes beyond the purview of this program

 

 

 

 

 

Program Overview

 

Thank you for your interest to participate in Python Practice Labs Program. DXC has developed this program to offers an environment where you can access live IT systems in real-time. We use real Cisco hardware and virtualized Windows, Linux and UNIX operating systems hosted on VMware and Hyper-V, all accessible via a web browser

 

Practice Labs makes it easy for you to put learning into practice in a safe environment that you can access anytime with a compatible PC, Browser and Internet connection.

 

The Program consists of a variety of learning components designed to provide you with the knowledge and skills needed to apply your learning on job environment. The program follows a blended approach, combining traditional self-directed online learning with Practice Labs

 

The program consists of the following:

 

       DXC University (self-paced computer-based training)

       Python Practice Labs

       Real time review of codes

       Assessments and Practice Exercises

       Progress tracking and reporting

       Ongoing support

 

The Program Manager will communicate with participants as required and will provide regular status reports.

 

Participant Commitments

 

       Allocate the necessary hours per week to participate and study (approximately 6 to 7 hours per week; varies by individual depending on experience and learning style)

       Complete all online training, if you are SME in this area then you can test yourself out of the online course

       Complete the Practice Labs

       Utilize recommended/supplemental learning assets

       Allocate the necessary study hours

       Get help when needed from the Support Team

 

 

 

Program Logistics

 

       All assets have been selected to assist on job application of learning

       You will find the weekly schedule details further on in this guide. If you done with week 1, you could move to week 2, week 3 & so on.

 

 

Participant Performance Tracking

Participants performance in this program will be tracked weekly. Results will be submitted to participants management.

 

 

Roles and Responsibilities

Participants Role:

       Complete all program requirements

       Complete all the surveys & assessments on time.

 

Program Support Team:

       Your Manager Provide encouragement and support to facilitate your successful completion of the program

       Support Team - Monitor your progress, prompt you to stay on schedule, provide progress reports, assist you with your questions and issues

 

DXC University Technical Support for Practice Labs

 

The Help & Support tab on the lab environment hosts resources designed to assist you in solving most lab issues you may encounter. In the event that the information in Help & Support does not resolve the issue, you can create a support ticket on the practice lab environment

 

You can also email our support team directly at support@practice-labs.com
