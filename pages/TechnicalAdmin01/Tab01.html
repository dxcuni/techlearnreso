  • Training: Complete training

  • Technical Career Path: Nominated by manager into program
  • Community Contribution & Collaboration: Present at Tech Forums and/or external conferences. Participate in cross-division, multi-function teams and writes white papers. Lead a HPE Java Technology Profession topic
